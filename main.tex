\section{Construction Exercise 2.12}
Draw rectangle OKAY with OK=7 and KA=5.
Solution

The vertices of rectangle OKAY are
\begin{align}
\vec{O}= \myvec{0\\0},\vec{K}= \myvec{a\\0},\vec{A}= \myvec{0\\c},\vec{Y}= \myvec{a\\c}
\end{align}
\begin{align}
\Longrightarrow\vec{O}= \myvec{0\\0},\vec{K}= \myvec{7\\0},\vec{A}= \myvec{0\\5},\vec{Y}= \myvec{7\\5}
\end{align}
Where a=7,c=5.\\
    In rectangle  OKAY \\OK=AY=7.\\
    OY=KA=5.
